#Aquarium Planner

This web application will provide a central hub for
users to build or upgrade aquariums for their home or business and provide component compatibility comparisons, allowing users to compile their own creations within a virtual environment. Additionally, Aquarium Planner will provide a list of marine plants and animals, providing the same compatibility and comparisons.

## Goals

- Estimate budgets
- Descriptions of products, components, and aquarium inhabitants
- Check compatibility


## Roadmap

- Options for users to purchase selected products
- AI to view aquarium build within users chosen placement
- Chat options for users to share experience with fish/products
- Water Chemistry
- Example builds from users and media
- News related to market or research in aquarium maintenance
